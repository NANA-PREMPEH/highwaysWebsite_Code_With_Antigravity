Ghana Highway Authority

The Ghana Highway Authority was established as a body corporate under NRC Decree 298 in December 1974. The NRCD 298 was repealed by GHA Act 1997 (Act 540 ) which limited the Authority's responsibility to the administration, control, development and maintenance of the country's trunk road network totalling 15,000 km and related facilities in Ghana.
